What is Vincent OS?

Vincent OS is a GNU/Linux distribution who was developped by v38armageddon.



What are features of Vincent OS?

Vincent OS include by default:

  • Custom Kernel.
  • Windows Application Compatibility.
  • Arch Linux based distribution.
  • AUR activated.
  • Unique desktop.

You can also install additional software if you don't like the minimum configuration:

  • Office configuration.
  • Gaming configuration.
  • Media configuration.
